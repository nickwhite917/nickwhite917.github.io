Who I am

My name is Nick White. Last year, my team (the Cerebro Project) won first place in the Senior Design Symposium. I learned many things from this experience - but most importantly, I learned how to present my ideas.

In addition to being a recent graduate, I am also a startup founder and a professional data engineer. I am the founder of Occamware Inc. (software consulting), Safekey Inc. (student safety via Internet of Things devices), and a data engineer at Parker Hannifin Corporation.
